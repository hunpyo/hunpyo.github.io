Research

Innovative Soft Systems

Exploring the intersection of soft robotics, flexible electronics, and smart materials to create intelligent systems capable of active deformation.

Project 01

Self-bendable Film

Combining shape memory alloy (SMA) wires with self-bendable substrates enables reversible bending and unbending under electrical control, providing a platform for flexible electronics.

Published in Soft Robotics, 2018 View Paper →
Project 02

Multi-channel Bendable Film

Combining locally bent SMA wires with flexible films featuring metal traces allows control of actuation regions and bending directions through electrical input, with adjustable curvature.

Published in Soft Robotics, 2022 (Cover Image) View Paper →
Project 03

Soft Robotic Flatworm

A multi-channel robot that moves in desired directions through reinforcement learning-based control strategies, mimicking biological tissue and brain structure.

Project 04

Multi-channel Morphing Sheet

Combining dielectric elastomer actuators (DEA) with multi-channel metal pads on a film enables shape morphing control through voltage input.
